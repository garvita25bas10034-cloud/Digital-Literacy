| DO’s | DON’Ts |
|------|--------|
| Use privacy settings to control who can see your personal life. | Don’t share personal information like your phone number, home address, or daily routine publicly. |
| Keep your personal profiles private and review your privacy settings regularly on every platform. | Don’t plagiarize or steal content without credit. |
| Report harmful, abusive, or fake content using the platform’s built-in reporting tools. | Don’t accept follow requests from suspicious or bot-like accounts. |
| Use platform features like Close Friends or viewer selectors to control who sees your content. | Don’t use social media during lectures, labs, or group study sessions as it disrupts both you and others. |
| Use a professional profile picture and a clean bio on platforms like LinkedIn that recruiters may view. | Don’t tag others in embarrassing photos without their explicit permission. |
| Treat others with the same respect you would in a face-to-face meeting. | Don’t engage in cyberbullying or trolling. |
| Turn off non-essential notifications during study hours to maintain focus and reduce distraction. | Don’t click on suspicious links or unknown messages. |
